Nor were we filmed in person during Techorama 2026.
But you do can still enjoy our presentation as a radio play accompanied with some slides when you have access to this year's [Techorama TV recordings](https://techorama.tv/collections/8798089d-c60e-4e45-88c5-4c04e88ec02d/play/9fd6770d-dd5b-4b32-8799-6b3e313f09c4){:target="_blank"}.

This year we joined the party with a full hour session "What the Scrolls Didn’t Say: A Real-World Microsoft Agent Framework Quest". Together with my colleague Frederiek Vandepitte we shared our heroes story about how we used the Microsoft Agent Framework in .NET as a proof of concept for a customer to create a hosted agentic workflow solution and made it available over different protocols.

It also covered the benefits of using Microsoft.Extensions.AI and the challenges you could still face with Microsoft Agent Framework at that time, but with a solution included.

We are doing a lot these days with Microsoft.Extensions.AI and the Microsoft Agent Framework. So expect some more posts or sessions about these subjects in the future!
